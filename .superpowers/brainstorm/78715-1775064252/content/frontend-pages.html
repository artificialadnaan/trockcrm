Frontend Design Decisions

  • Role-based routing: Sidebar nav items and routes filtered by user role. Reps don't see admin/director sections. Directors see both rep and director views.
  • Office switcher: Directors/admins see office dropdown in sidebar. Switching office changes the API context (different office JWT claim or header). Reps locked to their office.
  • Global search: Command palette (Cmd+K) searches across deals, contacts, and files using PG full-text search. Results grouped by entity type.
  • Notification bell: Badge count from SSE stream. Click opens notification center with deep links to relevant records.
  • Mobile bottom nav: Top 5 actions always accessible. "More" expands to full nav. Touch targets minimum 44x44px per your guidelines.
  • Charts: Recharts for all visualizations (per your stack conventions). Pie charts for pipeline distribution, bar charts for activity/performance, line charts for trends.
  • Pipeline Kanban: Closed Won and Closed Lost are NOT drag columns — they're separate sections. Prevents accidental terminal stage drops.
  • Lost deal modal: Triggered automatically when deal moves to Closed Lost. Reason dropdown + notes textarea. Cannot be dismissed without completing both fields.
